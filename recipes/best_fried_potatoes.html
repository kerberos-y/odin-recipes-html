Best Fried Eggs

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: herbs or spices for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  2. Once the butter is melted and starts to bubble, crack the eggs into the skillet.
  3. Cook the eggs for about 2-3 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny. For firmer yolks, cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Optional: Garnish with herbs or spices of your choice.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
